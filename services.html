CONTAINER DELIVERY

For light aircraft. A low-cost and very safe alternative to a risky and lengthy ferry flight.

Is container transport optimal? – I determine that it is for the mission, and/or the customer requests a container quote.

Port identification – I select cities to be used, considering cost, availability, etc.

Fly or truck – The method of moving the aircraft from its present location to the container-izer is determined. Ideally, the aircraft is airworthy and can be flown; if not it will need to be trucked to the container-izer.

Services after port arrival – Typically, my mission ends with delivery at the destination port. However, upon customer request I can add other services such as ground transportation in the destination country, and re-assembly.
